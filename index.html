Our Story

Where Korean BBQ Meets Vietnamese Street Food

Merge Cafe started with a simple idea: bring a fusion dining experience to the heart of Virginia that nobody had seen before. As a family business, we opened our first location in Quantico in 2016, steps from the Marine Corps Base.

Three years later, we expanded to Stafford because the community kept asking for more. Every banh mi is made fresh, every boba tea is handcrafted, and every customer feels like family.

Our Journey

From Quantico to Stafford

2016

The Beginning

Opened our first location on Potomac Ave in Quantico, VA. Steps from the Marine Corps Base, serving the military community with fusion sandwiches and fresh bubble tea.

2017

Building Our Menu

Introduced the Bulgogi Banh Mi, blending Korean BBQ with Vietnamese street food. It became our signature dish overnight. Expanded the boba menu to 15+ flavors.

2019

Stafford Opens

Community demand brought us to 44 Mine Rd in Stafford. Larger space, same family values, and extended evening hours for the growing dinner crowd.

2024

22+ Flavors & Growing

Over 150 five-star reviews, 22+ boba flavors, signature drinks like the Moon Cloud and Peach Rose Green Tea. Still family-run. Still making every order fresh.
